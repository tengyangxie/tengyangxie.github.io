Tengyang Xie

Hi! I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UMass Amherst, working with Gerome Miklau and Philip Thomas. My research interests lie broadly in the topics of machine learning, optimization, and statistics, such as reinforcement learning, nonconvex optimization, differential privacy, and fairness. My recent research includes risk-sensitive reinforcement learning, statistical foundation of off-policy reinforcement learning, and fairness.

I am delighted to complement my research with industrial experiences. I spent a wonderful summer working as a research intern at Amazon AI Palo Alto in 2018, with Yu-Xiang Wang and Yifei Ma.

Prior to UMass, I was an undergrad in Physics at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).
